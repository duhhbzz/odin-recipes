White Cheese Lasagna

Prep Time:

25 mins

Cook Time:

50 mins

Total Time:

1 hrs 15 mins

Servings:

12

Yield:

1 9x13-inch lasagna

Tired of ground beef and red sauce based lasagna? This White Cheese Chicken Lasagna will surely please your taste buds. The recipe is satisfying and a crowd-pleasing twist on a classic Italian favorite. The combination of white cheeses and chicken will be sure to bring smiles to your dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Butter
  • Vegetables
    • Onion
    • Frozen Spinach
  • Garlic
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Chicken Broth
  • Whole Milk
  • Herbs & Spices
    • Salt
    • Dried Basil
    • Dried Oregano
    • Black Pepper
    • Fresh Parsley
  • Cheese
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
    • Ricotta
  • Chicken

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes until cooked;drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat; stir in onion and garlic. Whisk in flour until lightly browned and onion is tender, about 2 minutes. Add chicken broth, milk, and salt; cook, whisking continuously, for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in 2 cups mozzarella and 1/4 cup Parmesan until well combined. Season with basil, oregano, and black pepper; remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Spread 1/3 of the sauce mixture in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, ricotta, and chicken. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles over the chicken and layer with 1/3 of the sauce mixture, spinach, remaining 2 cups mozzarella, and 1/2 cup Parmesan. Arrange remaining noodles over cheese and spread remaining sauce evenly over noodles. Sprinkle with parsley and remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
